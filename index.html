About UPSC

The UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) is one of the toughest and most prestigious exams in India, conducted to select candidates for top government services like IAS, IPS, IFS, and more. It consists of three stages: Prelims (objective), Mains (descriptive), and Interview (personality test). Candidates must be graduates and meet age and attempt criteria. The exam covers diverse subjects like History, Polity, Economy, and Current Affairs. Success requires consistent preparation, deep understanding, and strong analytical skills. Clearing UPSC offers an opportunity to serve the nation and make a lasting impact on society.

Why UPSC?

The UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) offers a unique opportunity to serve the nation and bring about positive change in society. As an IAS, IPS, or IFS officer, you get to work on policy implementation, public administration, and decision-making at the highest levels. The job provides prestige, authority, and job security, along with the power to address social issues and improve the lives of millions.
